Alfa Laval T8

Gasketed plate heat exchanger for a wide range of applications

Introduction

Alfa Laval Industrial line is a wide product range that is used in virtually all types of industry. Suitable for a wide range of applications, this model is available with a large selection of plate and gasket types.

Applications

  • Biotech and Pharmaceutical
  • Chemicals
  • Energy and Utilities
  • Food, Dairy and Beverages
  • HVAC and Refrigeration
  • Machinery and Manufacturing
  • Marine and Transportation
  • Mining, Minerals and Pigments
  • Pulp and Paper
  • Semiconductor and Electronics
  • Steel

Benefits

  • High energy efficiency - low operating cost
  • Flexible configuration - heat transfer area can be modified
  • Easy to install - compact design
  • High serviceability - easy to open for inspection and cleaning and easy to clean by CIP
  • Access to Alfa Laval's global service network
